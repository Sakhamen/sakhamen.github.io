Location
Mthatha, South Africa

Residential

Here we have a large two-storey house with a pool in the backyard. The house is surrounded by lush green grass and has several palm trees growing nearby, there are also some plants near the stone wall that runs along one side of the property. At the front of the house, there is a porch with stairs leading up to it and several windows on either side of the entrance door. The sky above is bright blue with white clouds scattered throughout, adding to its beauty. Inside, there is likely furniture such as beds and pillows visible through one window, giving us an idea of what lies within this home’s walls. This picture simply captures a peaceful moment in time where one can appreciate all that nature has to offer while still enjoying modern comforts like pools and homes for shelter from life’s storms.

The house has the following:
  • Open plan of a lounge, dining and kitchen (with scullery)
  • Entertainment area with a bar
  • Cinema room
  • 3 Bedrooms (2 with en-suites)
  • Bathroom (Shower, bath, basin & WC)
  • Master Bedroom with a dresser and en-suite
  • Study room and Pyjama lounge
  • Double garage
  • Outdoor chilling area with Swimming pool
  • Outbuilding
